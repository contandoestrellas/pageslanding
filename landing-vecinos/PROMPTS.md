## üìã AI Tool Recommendations

### Best Platforms for Food/Product Photography:

1. **Midjourney** - Best for: Highly detailed, artistic food photography and styling
   - Use: `/imagine` command with long detailed prompts
   - Quality: Very high, consistent styling
   - Cost: Subscription-based (effective)

2. **DALL-E 3** - Best for: Commercial product photography, quick generation
   - Use: Direct text prompts in ChatGPT
   - Quality: Good commercial quality
   - Cost: Pay-per-image

3. **Leonardo.ai** - Best for: Food photography with fine details
   - Use: Detailed prompts + fine-tuned models
   - Quality: High detail, photorealistic
   - Cost: Credits system

4. **Stable Diffusion** (via RunwayML) - Best for: Custom training, batch generation
   - Use: Advanced controls and inpainting
   - Quality: Very customizable
   - Cost: Various options
